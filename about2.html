About Us

UNHCR-Exchange is a web plaform designed for people fleeing conflict, and those working to ease their journey

An initiative of the United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees (UNHCR), Exchange is purpose-built to serve the need of both refugees abd practitioners, through:

Online Courses

A catalogue of online courses which, combined with UNHCR’s Community Technology Access centres, provide a blended learning experience to assist refugees to gain the skills needed to sustain themselves - both in transition and once settled.

Find out more

Interactive Virtual Communities

A number of interactive virtual communities for refugees, support practitioners and UNHCR associates. These communities will enable friends and colleagues to share, discuss, and learn.

Find out more

Resource Library

A Resource Repository that enables the efficient sharing and distribution of important digital materials in even the most challenging of environments.

Find out more

Employment Tools

A series of employment tools to assist refugees in both employment and entrepreneurship. Customized to each participant, these include credentials and certifications, as well as services to match refugees to career paths and business opportunities,

Find out more
