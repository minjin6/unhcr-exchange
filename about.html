About the platform, app and
Communities of Learning and Practice

UNHCR Exchange is a tool and methodology to assist you in running successful learning and collaboration projects through two way communications between refugees and practitioners.

Exchange offers online communities of practice and learning to accompany your face to face learning and collaboration projects in areas such as:

  • Capacity Building Projects, including continuous peer to peer coaching
  • Learning programmes for refugees
  • Collaborative Learning experiences between refugees and practitioners
  • Innovation projects and idea generation
  • Exchange of Best Practice
  • Staff training (for UNHCR Staff and implementing partners )

The system runs on computers (in browser), tablets and smartphones (in the app). Besides allowing for easy access to content, Exchange emphasises user generated content: Users can upload any type of media and comment on each otherâ€™s content to further ideas in collaboration.

The Exchange experience works best when combining online and face to face experiences. The platform then becomes the assistive learning Tool to enhance a specific learning programme.
